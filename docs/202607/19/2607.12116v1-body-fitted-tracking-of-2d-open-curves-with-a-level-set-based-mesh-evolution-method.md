## 摘要
本文描述了一种用于追踪二维空间中一组开曲线运动的新型数值算法。所提策略在演化过程的每次迭代中结合了这些曲线的两种互补表示：一方面，它们作为整个计算域网格实体的子集被显式网格化；同时，使用水平集方法的一种变体，它们被隐式地捕捉为两个辅助标量函数的负、零和正子集的代数组合，这两个函数定义在整个环境空间上。这种表示的耦合允许在这些曲线上执行精确的几何或力学计算，同时为其形状的大幅演化留出空间。在描述其主要数值成分之后，提出了该方法论的几个应用示例，用于模拟开放物理间断的运动，如涡层卷起，并优化开放曲线的形状，例如相对于其各向异性长度，旨在改善增材制造背景下激光作用于粉末床的轨迹，或重建裂缝集。

## Abstract
This article describes a novel numerical algorithm for tracking the motion of a collection of open curves in two space dimensions. The proposed strategy combines two complementary representations of these curves at each iteration of the evolution process: on the one hand, they are meshed explicitly, as a sub-collection of the entities of a mesh of the total computational domain. Concurrently, using a variant of the Level Set Method, they are captured implicitly as algebraic combinations of the negative, zero and positive subsets of two auxiliary scalar functions, defined on the whole ambient space. This coupling of representations allows to perform accurate geometric or mechanical computations on these curves, while leaving room for large evolution of their shape. After the description of its main numerical ingredients, several applications examples of this methodology are proposed, where it is used to simulate the motion of open physical discontinuities, such as a vortex sheet roll-up, and to optimize the shape of open-ended curves, e.g. with respect to their anisotropic length, with the aim to improve the trajectory of a laser acting on a powder bed in the context of additive manufacturing, or to reconstruct fracture sets.
