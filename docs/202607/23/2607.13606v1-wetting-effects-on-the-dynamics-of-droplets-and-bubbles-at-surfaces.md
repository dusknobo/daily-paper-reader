## 摘要
动态润湿通过影响接触线运动和界面演化，在固体表面上液滴与气泡的动力学中起着基础性作用。本文采用三维流体体积法框架，结合最近发展的基于接触线速度重构方法的动态润湿模型[19]，研究了三个代表性的润湿控制基准问题：液滴飞溅、固体表面气泡聚并以及剪切流下的气泡动力学。模型首先通过液滴飞溅和气泡聚并的实验观察与文献数据进行了验证，准确再现了瞬态接触线演化、飞溅形态和聚并动力学。特别是，动态润湿抑制了静态润湿模型预测的气泡过早脱离，与实验观察的一致性显著改善。在剪切流中，接触角滞后和接触线耗散强烈影响气泡的变形、滑移和脱离。这些结果表明，准确处理动态润湿对于预测涉及快速接触线运动、强烈界面变形和拓扑变化的润湿控制液滴与气泡至关重要。

## Abstract
Dynamic wetting plays a fundamental role in the dynamics of droplets and bubbles at solid surfaces by influencing contact line motion and interfacial evolution. In this work, three representative wetting-controlled benchmarks, namely droplet splashing, bubble coalescence at solid surfaces, and bubble dynamics under shear flow, are investigated using a three-dimensional volume-of-fluid framework coupled with a recently developed dynamic wetting model based on contact line velocity reconstruction method [19]. The model is first validated against experimental observations and literature data for droplet splashing and bubble coalescence. It accurately reproduces the transient contact line evolution, splashing morphology, and coalescence dynamics. In particular, dynamic wetting suppresses the premature bubble detachment predicted by static wetting models and yields substantially improved agreement with experimental observations. In shear flow, contact angle hysteresis and contact line dissipation strongly influence bubble deformation, sliding, and detachment. These results demonstrate that accurate treatment of dynamic wetting is essential for predicting wetting-controlled droplets and bubbles involving rapid contact line motion, strong interfacial deformation, and topology changes.
